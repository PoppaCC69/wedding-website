Getting Here

Travel

By Tube

Bethnal Green (Central line) is a 15-minute walk — a direct, easy journey from anywhere in central London.

By Uber or Cab

We're right in the heart of East London — Ubers are plentiful all evening. This is our top recommendation for the journey home after a big night.

By Car

Leave it at home! Street parking in E2 is limited and you'll enjoy the evening far more without worrying about it.

Good to Know

FAQs

Summer Formal — think elegant, polished and a little bit chic. Linen suits, floral midi dresses, statement accessories. Smart and sophisticated is the vibe. Please kindly avoid white or very light cream.

We absolutely adore your little ones, but our wedding is an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you the perfect excuse for a night off — we'll raise a glass to the kids!

We've kept our guest list intentional and personal. Please check your invitation to see if a plus one is included — and feel free to reach out directly if you're unsure.

We'd love for you to be fully present during the ceremony — phones away, please! Our photographer will capture every moment. You're very welcome to snap away at the reception.

Guests are welcome from 2:00pm. The ceremony begins at 2:30pm sharp — please aim to be seated by 2:20pm. We'd hate for you to miss a moment.

Your presence is honestly our greatest gift. If you'd like to contribute to our honeymoon fund, details will follow closer to the time — but truly, no pressure at all.

There are great hotels in Shoreditch, Bethnal Green and the wider East London area. We'd recommend searching around E1 and E2 for options within easy walking distance. We'll share recommendations closer to the time.

We'd strongly recommend leaving the car at home. Central London with great tube and Uber links means there's really no need — enjoy the evening and let someone else do the driving.
