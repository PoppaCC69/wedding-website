We've kept our guest list intentional and personal. Please check your invitation to see if a plus one is included — and feel free to reach out directly if you're unsure.

Your presence on our day is the greatest gift we could ask for. If you'd like to give something, cash is always gratefully received — but truly, no pressure at all.

We'd love for you to be fully present during the ceremony — phones away, please! Our photographer will capture every moment. You're very welcome to snap away at the reception.

Day guests are welcome from 2:00pm — the ceremony begins at 2:30pm sharp, so please aim to be seated by 2:20pm. Evening guests are welcome from 7:00pm.

We'd strongly recommend leaving the car at home. Central London with great tube and Uber links means there's really no need — enjoy the evening and let someone else do the driving.
