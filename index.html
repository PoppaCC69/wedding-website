Getting Here

Travel

By Tube

Bethnal Green (Central line) is a 15-minute walk, a direct and easy journey from anywhere in central London.

By Uber or Cab

We're right in the heart of East London. Ubers are plentiful all evening and our top recommendation for the journey home after a big night.

Leave it at home! Street parking in E2 is limited and you'll enjoy the evening far more without worrying about it.
